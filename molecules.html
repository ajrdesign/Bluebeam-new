Nesting Columns

Nesting columns is easy, you can add a "row" inside another "row" or use "row-fluid" fluid to break up the section into twelve smaller sections.

<div class="span4">
    <div class="row-fluid">
      <div class="span6">
      ...
      </div>
      <div class="span6">
      ...
      </div>
    </div>
</div>
